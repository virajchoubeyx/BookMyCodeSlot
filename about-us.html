About Us

Who We Are

At BookMyCodeSlot, we are a team of passionate computer science graduates who understand firsthand the challenges of breaking into the tech industry. With our combined experiences, we’ve crafted a platform that not only teaches you to code but also prepares you for the rigors of securing your dream job. Our mission is to empower you with the skills and confidence to achieve your goals in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Meet the Founder

Hello! I’m Viraj Choubey, the founder and Chief Executive Officer of BookMyCodeSlot. As a fellow computer science graduate, I’ve navigated the complexities of the job market and understand what it takes to stand out. This platform is born out of my desire to share the insights I’ve gained and help others succeed. Together with my team, I’m committed to guiding you on your journey to a successful career in tech.

The Story

We are a group of graduates from a tier 3 college who deeply understand the unique challenges that come with it. Our journey has been shaped by experiences that many students from similar backgrounds face, and we are here to share what we've learned to help you secure your first job, whether it's through on-campus placements or finding opportunities off-campus.

Speaking for myself, I was fortunate to get placed early in my placement season. However, the decisions I made afterward led to a long wait for the joining letters from the companies I was placed in. During this waiting period, with only 170 rupees in my bank account, the idea for this venture took root. It was a risky decision, but one driven by a desire to show the world that students from tier 3 colleges can also turn their thoughts into reality.
